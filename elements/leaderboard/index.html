February 18 – 20, 2022

A virtual gaming event to support keeping families close

Yahtzee Leaderboard

Try to reach the top!

During Givers & Gamers, play a game of Yahtzee and submit your score. The player with the highest score will win a $25 gift card of his or her choice. Enter as many times as you like; only your highest score will be counted.

You may play online at Board Game Arena or at home. You need only five, 6-sided dice and a way to keep score. If you have a printer, here's a scorecard you can use. Rules are available at OfficialGameRules.org and Wikipedia.

Players must be registered Givers & Gamers participants in order to qualify for prizes. A Google Account is required to submit entries. Each entry must contain a link to a BGA table or a photo of your scorecard. The deadline for entry is 3:30pm EST Sunday, February 20, 2022.
