February 19 – 21, 2021

A virtual gaming event to support keeping families close

Yahtzee Leaderboard

Try to reach the top!

During Givers & Gamers, play a game of Yahtzee and submit your score. The players with the top 3 scores will each win a $25 giftcard of their choice.

You may play online at Board Game Arena or at home. You need only five, 6-sided dice and a way to keep score. If you have a printer, here's a scorecard you can use. Rules are available at OfficialGameRules.org and Wikipedia.

Players must be registered Givers & Gamers participants in order to qualify for prizes. A Google Account is required to submit entries. Each entry must contain a link to a BGA table or a photo of your scorecard. The deadline for entry is 3:30pm EST Sunday, February 21, 2021.
